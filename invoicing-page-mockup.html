YOUR INVOICING

Everything you need to get paid

Branded, professional PDFs

Every invoice generates a clean PDF with your logo, colours, and company details. Download it, attach it to an email, or let neno send it for you.

Shareable payment pages

Each invoice gets a unique payment link your client can open in their browser. They see exactly what they owe and your bank details, and can pay instantly.

Invoice activity timeline

See exactly what happened and when. Created, sent, viewed, paid — every event is logged so you never have to wonder about the status of an invoice.

Recurring invoices

Set up invoices that repeat on a schedule: weekly, monthly, or custom. Neno generates and sends them automatically, so you get paid on time without remembering to bill.

Automatic payment matching

When a client pays, neno recognises the transaction and marks the invoice as paid — instantly. No manual reconciliation, no checking your bank. You just get notified that the money has arrived.

Smart payment reminders

Overdue invoices get follow-ups automatically. Set the tone, set the schedule, and let neno handle the uncomfortable conversations so you can focus on your work.
