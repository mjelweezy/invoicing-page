FAQ

Do I need a neno business account to use accounting?

No. Neno accounting works with any Dutch bank account. A business account with neno enables us to automate away more admin which means less manual work for you and faster reconciliation.

What's included in the monthly fee?

All core accounting services: quarterly VAT filings, annual accounts, corporate tax returns, and quarterly closes. You also get direct chat access to your dedicated accountant. No hourly billing, no surprise invoices.

What about payroll?

Payroll administration is a separate service. Let us know if you need it, and we can discuss adding it to your plan.

How quickly can I get started?

Most businesses are fully onboarded within a week. Book a call, and we'll walk you through the process.

What if I have an existing accountant?

We can help with the transition. Your Neno accountant will work with you to gather historical records and ensure a smooth handover.

Do you handle the 30% ruling or other expat tax matters?

For employee income tax (IB) and the 30% ruling, we can connect you with a specialist. Neno accounting focuses on corporate BV finances—VAT, VPB, and annual accounts.
