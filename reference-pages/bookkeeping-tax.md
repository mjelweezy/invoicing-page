### Section: "FAQ"

**Q: Do I need a neno business account to use accounting?**
A: No. Neno accounting works with any Dutch bank account. A business account with neno enables us to automate away more admin which means less manual work for you and faster reconciliation.

**Q: What's included in the monthly fee?**
A: All core accounting services: quarterly VAT filings, annual accounts, corporate tax returns, and quarterly closes. You also get direct chat access to your dedicated accountant. No hourly billing, no surprise invoices.

**Q: What about payroll?**
A: Payroll administration is a separate service. Let us know if you need it, and we can discuss adding it to your plan.

**Q: How quickly can I get started?**
A: Most businesses are fully onboarded within a week. Book a call, and we'll walk you through the process.

**Q: What if I have an existing accountant?**
A: We can help with the transition. Your Neno accountant will work with you to gather historical records and ensure a smooth handover.

**Q: Do you handle the 30% ruling or other expat tax matters?**
A: For employee income tax (IB) and the 30% ruling, we can connect you with a specialist. Neno accounting focuses on corporate BV finances—VAT, VPB, and annual accounts.
