### Section: "FAQ"

**Q: How long does incorporation take?**
A: Standard: 20 working days. Fast: 7 working days. Urgent: 3 working days. The clock starts once you've submitted your details and completed identity verification.

**Q: Do I need to visit a notary in person?**
A: No. Identity verification happens via a short video call. You won't need to visit a notary office.

**Q: What's included in the price?**
A: Everything: notarial deed of incorporation (including articles of association), shareholder register, KVK registration, digital company extract, and UBO register filing. The KVK invoices a separate €82.25 registration fee directly to your BV.

**Q: Is there a minimum starting capital?**
A: Since 2012, there's no €18,000 requirement. You can incorporate with as little as €0.01. We default to €1.20 (120 shares of €0.01) for flexibility if you later want to issue or transfer shares.

**Q: Can I register my BV at an address other than my home?**
A: The initial registration must use your residential address. After incorporation, you can change the address at the KVK for free.

**Q: Do I need to register with the KVK myself?**
A: No. We handle the Chamber of Commerce registration. You'll receive your KVK number and digital extract once the BV is incorporated.

**Q: Can I incorporate multiple BVs at once?**
A: Yes. You can incorporate a holding and an operating company (werkmaatschappij) together, or multiple BVs simultaneously.

**Q: Are the incorporation costs tax-deductible?**
A: Yes. Costs incurred to establish a business—including incorporation fees—are deductible as startup expenses.

**Q: What is identity verification (legalisatie)?**
A: A legal requirement under Dutch anti-money laundering law (Wwft). The notary verifies your identity via video call using your passport or ID card. Takes a few minutes.

**Q: Can I incorporate a BV without a notary?**
A: No. Dutch law requires a notary to execute the deed of incorporation. We handle the notary relationship—you don't need to find or contact one yourself.
