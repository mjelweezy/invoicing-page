### Section: "FAQ"

**Q: How long does it take to open an account?**
A: We aim to have your account ready in as little as 3 hours. For some customers, the process can take up to 3 days, but we're here to help answer your questions at any time.

**Q: Will I get a Dutch IBAN?**
A: Yes. You receive a local NL IBAN that works everywhere—pay suppliers, receive payments, set up direct debits.

**Q: Is my money safe?**
A: Your funds are held in segregated accounts, separate from operational funds, and protected up to €100,000 under the EU Deposit Guarantee Scheme.

**Q: Who can open an account?**
A: Any Dutch BV with at least one director who is an EEA resident. We support most Dutch legal entities including holdings, VOFs, and foundations.

**Q: Can I add team members?**
A: Yes. Add employees or your accountant with custom permissions. Issue additional cards as needed.

**Q: How do I make international payments?**
A: Transfer to 160+ countries directly from your account. Exchange rates and fees are shown upfront before you confirm.

**Q: Is neno a bank?**
A: Neno partners with Swan for payment services. Swan is an Electronic Money Institution regulated by the French ACPR and registered with De Nederlandsche Bank.

**Q: Can I use this as my primary business account?**
A: Absolutely. Receive payments, pay suppliers, set up direct debits, and manage cards—everything you need from a business account.
