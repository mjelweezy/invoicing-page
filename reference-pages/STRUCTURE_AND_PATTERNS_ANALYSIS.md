# neno.co Product Marketing Pages - Structure and Patterns Analysis

## Overview
This document analyzes the structure, content patterns, and design approaches across three neno product marketing pages to identify consistent patterns and unique positioning.

---

## Page Structure Comparison

### Common Header Elements (All Pages)
- neno logo (navigation link)
- Navigation menu: Product | Manifesto | Pricing | Resources
- Language selector (English)
- Log in link
- "Get started" CTA button (blue)

### Hero Section (All Pages)
**Consistent Elements:**
- Product label/badge at top (Business Accounts, BV Incorporation, Bookkeeping & Tax)
- Bold, large headline
- Descriptive subheading
- Two CTA buttons: "See pricing" + "Book a demo" or "Start incorporation"
- Hero imagery (professional/founder lifestyle photos)

**Visual Balance:**
- Left: Text content
- Right: Hero image or interactive mockup

---

## Content Architecture Patterns

### Three-Pillar Section Structure
All three pages use a consistent "3-pillar" content pattern:

**Business Accounts & Cards:**
1. IBAN in as little as 3 hours
2. Local and international payments
3. Bookkeeping, payroll & tax built in, done on time

**BV Incorporation:**
1. Fast-track incorporation
2. Complete from anywhere
3. All-inclusive pricing

**Bookkeeping & Tax:**
1. Dutch tax expertise, built in
2. Complete context from day one
3. One team, one place

**Pattern:** The three pillars break down major benefits into digestible, distinct value propositions. Each pillar has a title and explanatory text.

## Pricing/Tier Presentation

### Business Accounts & Cards
**Approach:** Embedded within content, mentions "Professional accounting from €80.00/month" with description and benefits list

### BV Incorporation
**Approach:** Dedicated pricing tier section with 4 visible options:
- Standard (€449.00, 20 working days)
- Fast (€749.00, 7 working days) - MARKED AS "POPULAR"
- Urgent (€1049.00, 3 working days)
- BV + Holding (€798.00, 20 working days)

Each tier shows:
- Turnaround time
- Price (excl. BTW)
- "Begin" CTA button
- List of included features with checkmarks
- Upsells for each tier level

### Bookkeeping & Tax
**Approach:** Service-based pricing not prominently featured on marketing page (focuses on value prop instead)

**Pattern:**
- Accounts & Cards: Inline pricing mention, focuses on value
- BV Incorporation: Four-tier comparison with "Popular" badge and visual distinction
- Bookkeeping & Tax: Price-agnostic, emphasizes service quality

## Visual and Design Patterns

### Typography Hierarchy
- Large bold headings (main headlines)
- Section labels in small caps (e.g., "START YOUR BUSINESS", "SMART BOOKKEEPING")
- Numbered sections (1., 2., 3.)
- Consistent weight and size hierarchy

### Color Scheme
- Primary: Navy/black text
- Accent: Blue buttons (#5B5BFF or similar)
- Background: Alternating white and black sections
- Secondary: Gray text for descriptions

### Button Styling
- Primary CTAs: Blue rounded buttons
- Secondary CTAs: Outlined or text links
- Consistent button text: "Book a demo", "See pricing", "Begin", "Start incorporation"

### Photography
- Professional lifestyle imagery
- Diverse representation (multiple genders, ethnicities)
- Candid/authentic poses (not overly staged)
- Office/workspace settings
- Business casual attire

### Section Backgrounds
- Alternating white and dark (black) backgrounds
- Dark sections (black) typically contain process steps or key messaging
- Creates visual rhythm and breaks up content

## CTA Strategy

### Primary CTAs (Throughout Pages)
1. "Book a demo" - Discovery/education
2. "See pricing" - Economic evaluation
3. "Get started" - Account creation
4. "Start incorporation" - Service initiation
5. "Begin" - Tier selection (BV Incorporation)

### CTA Placement
- Hero section: Both "See pricing" and "Book a demo"
- Throughout content: "Book a demo" for education
- Pricing section: "Begin" for tier selection
- Final section: "Book a demo" again
- Footer: Typically includes primary CTA

### Pattern:** Multiple CTAs with different purposes (education vs. direct signup). "Book a demo" is heavily emphasized across all pages, suggesting this is a critical conversion step.

## Consistent Patterns Summary

1. **Hero Section:** All pages follow a consistent left-text, right-image layout with product label, headline, subheading, and dual CTAs

2. **Three-Pillar Benefits:** All pages break main value props into three digestible pillars

3. **Black Background Process Sections:** All pages have a dark background section explaining the customer journey

4. **Six Feature Cards:** All pages feature six service/product benefits in a grid layout

5. **Testimonial Strategy:** All pages use 6 customer testimonials with 5-star ratings, with strategic reuse across pages

6. **FAQs:** All pages include FAQ sections addressing common concerns

7. **CTA Buttons:** Consistent blue styling and placement throughout

8. **Alternating Backgrounds:** White and black sections create visual rhythm

9. **Professional Imagery:** Lifestyle photos of entrepreneurs and professionals

10. **Simplified Onboarding:** All pages show step-by-step customer journey (4-5 steps)

## Recommendations for Reference

These three pages demonstrate:
1. **Scalability:** Same template can work for different service types
2. **Flexibility:** Core structure adapts to product needs (pricing emphasis varies)
3. **Trust Building:** Consistent use of social proof and expertise signals
4. **Clarity:** Simple step-by-step processes reduce friction
5. **Conversion Focus:** Multiple CTAs with different customer journey stages
6. **Professional Presentation:** Consistent visual language builds brand coherence
